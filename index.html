Recipe for Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepperjack
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Cooking Instructions

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese to top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for two minutes
